Hi! My name is Shadiamond Walters and I am a 3rd-year Journalism student at Fordham University- Lincoln Center. On campus, I am the Coordinator of the Commuter First-Year Mentor Program and a student-worker in the Office of Undergraduate Admissions. I chose to take up Journalism as a profession because I believe that everyones story should be told accurately and effectively in order to create real change in the world. As a first generation college student and the daughter of immigrant parents I have witness first hand the importance of ethical journalist and I aspire to be one. This website includes more insight into who I am and the projects I have worked on thus far. I hope you enjoy!
